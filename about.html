About me

I'm an interdisciplinary designer working at the crossroads of technology, design and art. Over the last decade, I've had the chance to help companies validate, prototype and design digital products that help improve how we live today.

Originally from Surabaya,Indonesia, I decided to move to the US at the age of 12 for a better education and opportunities, but dearly miss my mom's Fried noodle and not worrying about taking an umbrella with me every single day.

My design philosophy is always asking "why" for any decision and making sure I am solving a problem, instead of just creating something beautiful. Design Sprints help me achieving this goal. In my work, I bring Kediri flavour, some humour and hopefully, a great style.

I also love giving talks. And talking.
